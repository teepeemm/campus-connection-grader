Instructions

  • (Opera only) First, open a single Blackboard Grade Center and switch to that tab for the remaining instructions.
  • Click the at the top right ⇗ to get started.
  • Click "Choose File" to see if a .csv file will upload properly to Campus Connection.
  • If a Blackboard Grade Center is open, choose "Download Grades" to download a .csv file that could be uploaded to Campus Connection.
  • Or choose "Transfer Grades" to see what grades will transfer directly from Blackboard to Campus Connection without saving a .csv file as an intermediary.
